About Us


Make Tea, not War

Welcome to Serenitea, where we believe in the power of tea to bring people together and create moments of serenity in our fast-paced lives. Our passion for tea has inspired us to create a variety of delicious milk tea blends that are made with high-quality ingredients and served with love.


At Serenitea, we understand that life can be hectic, but we believe that taking a few moments to enjoy a cup of tea can help bring balance and peace to your day. Our mission is to provide a sanctuary where you can escape the chaos and stress of daily life and find a moment of tranquility.


So come join us at Serenitea and Make Tea, Not War. Let's create moments of peace, serenity, and happiness together, one cup at a time.
